10. The Navigate LED is continuously ON, what does that mean?

This implies that Exploit Tracker is not connected to GPS and is unable to update location information.

11. The Navigate LED is blinking every second, what does that mean?

This implies that the Exploit Tracker is connected to GPS and is getting location information successfully.

12. The status LED is blinking every seconds, what does it mean?

This implies that Exploit Tracker device is functioning in normal mode.

23. How do I check daily alarms from my Drivemate?

Open the Exploit Tracker mobile application on your phone and navigate to Car Alarms section.

Daily alarm logs could be checked under this section.

24. How do I enable/disable SMS or Email alerts notifications from Drivemate?

Open the Exploit Tracker mobile application on your phone and navigate to Car Alarms - Alarm Settings(icon).

Notifications could be turned ON/OFF using the option provided against each alarm.

25. How do I create or deactivate any Geofence area alarms?

Open the Exploit Tracker mobile application on your phone and navigate to Car Alarms -> Alarm Settings(icon)-> +Geofence.

New areas could be created under this section. Using the checkbox against each geofence, entry and exit alerts for that area could be managed.

If the checkbox against any geofence area is un-checked then it will be treated as de-activated Geofence and no SMS/email notifications will be generated.

26. How do I change settings for Overspeed and Idle Alarms?

Open the Exploit Tracker mobile application on your phone and navigate to Car Alarms -> Alarm Settings(icon).

To change the Overspeed settings tap on "+Over Speeding" . Change the values and tap on save button.

To change the Idle settings tap on "+Idling" . Change the value and tap on save button.
